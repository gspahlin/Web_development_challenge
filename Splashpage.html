Weather Analysis by Latitude

How Does the Weather Change as One Approaches the Poles?

It might seem obvious that as one goes north towards the arctic or south towards the antarctic, things tend to cool down. Most people have had the experience of visiting a warm location to the south of them - whether it be a tropical location, or a state or city somewhat to the south. But how well does this actually hold? Should we be disturbed that warm, sunny Spain shares similar latitudes to the relatively cold Illinois? And are other weather patterns such as cloud cover or humidity related to latitude?

In order to answer these questions I have built a program to dial 500 - 600 weather stations around the planet to obtain current weather data. The data is then plotted using the Pandas and Matplotlib libraries of python. The plots and commentary can be found on the pages of this website.

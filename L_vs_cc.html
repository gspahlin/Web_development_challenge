The Relationship between Latitude and Cloud Cover

Cloud Cover Does Not Correlate with Latitude


Cloud cover does not show a significant correlation with latitude. This is not a surprise, as there is no reason to think it should. The plots for cloud cover show horizontal lines at certain percentages. This is likely explained by people estimating cloud cover, which leads to modal values at "round" figures like 75% or 90%.
